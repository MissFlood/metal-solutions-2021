    About ZM Silesia

    For 170 years ZM SILESIA SA, a member of the Impexmetal Group, has been a leading company specialising in zinc production processing and today are the only manufacturer of zinc-titanium as well as zinc and zinc alloy wires for metal spraying in Poland.

    ZM Silesia zinc is a modern material produced of the highest purity zinc SHG 99,995% with additions of copper and titanium. State of the art technology guaranties very good physical and mechanical properties and allows for wide range of applications in the building industry.

    ZM Silesia zinc is produced according to EN 988 standard, in three differing finishes; Natural, Pre-aged (preweathered) and Carbon (dark preweathered) and has a standard 30 years warranty.

    ZM Silesia Advantages

    • Durable for over 80 years.
    • Corrosion resistant.
    • Requires no maintenance.
    • May be easily formed and jointed.
    • Excellent for soldering.
    • Light and non-flammable.
    • 100% recyclable.
