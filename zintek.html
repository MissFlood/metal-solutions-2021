    About Zintek

    Zintek is an all-Italian company that produces and markets rolled zinc-titanium products for architectural use.

    Located in Porto Marghera Venice and active for over 80 years, the company offers a production chain that covers every step of the process from zinc casting to the production, sale and application of zinc.

    Today, the company, part of the Cordifin Group, is a reference point for roofing solutions, façades and tinsmithery systems suitable for any context. Zintek offers its clients a product of the highest quality, as well as consultancy and support from the design phase to the actual installation, to help use the characteristics of its product in the best way possible and to guarantee the beauty, long-lasting lifespan and authenticity of buildings, while also paying attention to their environmental sustainability.

    Zintek® is a high quality Zinc-Copper-Titanium alloy, fully compliant with European standard EN 988 “Zinc and Zinc alloys” requirements for rolled flat products for the construction industry. The alloy must be produced from Z1 quality 99.995 Zinc according to European standard EN 1179, with the addition of alloying elements.

    Environmental

    The transfer of zinc due to flowing rainwater causes no risk to the environment. Zinc used for roofing, cladding and other types of construction works is completely recyclable and re-usable in a variety of industries, and maintains a high market value even as scrap metal. Unlike other materials, Zintek® requires no maintenance.

    The Coloured Ones

    Always attentive to latest trends, Zintek introduces six colours that enhance the nature of zinc-titanium, while leaving its properties unaltered; Mediterranean Blue, Lagoon Green, Antique Red, Natural Earth Brown, Graphite Black & Rock Grey

    Choosing Zintek® means choosing the visible and tangible pleasure of a lively material, capable of lasting over time without losing its value.
