    Nordic Copper – The World´s Finest Copper Solutions.

    Copper was one of the first metals used by man and is one of our oldest building materials, with unique properties and characteristics. Nordic Copper stands for individual architectural solutions, a long life cycle for buildings and a responsible legacy for future generations.

    Recycled Raw Material

    All Aurubis architectural copper products are manufactured by 100% recycled raw-material. Copper’s ability to be recycled repeatedly, without any loss in performance, is an important sustainable benefit.

    Copper and the Environment

    Today, around 50% of Europe’s copper demand is met by recycled material. It has been estimated that at least 65% of all copper ever mined is still in use, or available for use, today, having been recycled over and over.

    Explore Nordic Copper

    Nordic Copper is the product range for architectural applications, e.g. facades and roofs, made by Aurubis. Available in standard copper, brass & bronze finshes. The unrivaled range of factory-applied surface treatments provides copper and brass with various stages of oxidation and patination immediately.

    Nordic Copper Special Patina is designed to help architects in renovation projects. We offer unique pre-patinated copper to match naturally patinated copper especially for historical buildings.

    Long traditions in renovation

    Aurubis Finland has several decades of experience in individual patination of architectural copper products, which are successfully applied on many heritage listed buildings.

    Sustainability for years to come

    All of our architectural copper products are manufactured by 100% recycled raw-material. Copper’s ability to be recycled repeatedly, without any loss in performance, is an important sustainable benefit. Original patina mixture is stored in case of the need for refollow-up orders.

    Nordic Décor is Aurubis's extensive product range, combining Nordic Copper with grindings and pattern embossings.

    Nordic Décor combines the range of pre-oxidised and pre-patinated copper with various mechanical surface treatments, e.g. grindings and pattern embossings. The material can be shaped, edged, milled, lasered, cut and bent.

    New freedom of design

    Nordic Décor can be used for indoors, e.g. in hotels for bars and counters, wall design, elevator cladding and ceilings, in external design as an eye-catcher and for facades. As a design element for roofing, it shines as a maintenance-free, storm- and rain-resistant building surface.

    Individual design

    In addition to the large number of Nordic Décor products, the type and intensity of the grinding and pattern embossing can be developed individually.

    Grindings

    The various Nordic Décor grindings for Nordic Standard, Nordic Brass, Nordic Bronze and Nordic Brown are applied onto one face of the copper sheets or coils and give the surface a rustic and grainy finish. The type and intensity of the grinding can be developed and adjusted individually.

    Embossings

    Nordic Décor embossings are available for all Nordic Copper products. The type and intensity of the embossings can be developed and adjusted individually. Pre-patinated surfaces with embossings can be grinded for additional design effects.

    Nordic Bronze
    Nordic Standard is mill finish copper without any additional surface treatments carried out at the production site. The material has the traditional bright finish that will oxidise in the environment. Nordic Standard can also be combined with other surface finishes, e.g. Nordic Bronze to create various visual effects. Ongoing changes will continue over time with all Nordic Products depending on the local environment. Within a few days of exposure to the atmosphere, the surface of Nordic Standard copper begins to oxidize, changing its color from the “bright” mill finish to a chestnut brown which gradually darkens over several years to a chocolate brown. Continued weathering can then result in development of the distinctive green patina – or blue in coastal locations.
    Nordic Brown products are burnished at the Aurubis mill to immediately provide the same oxidised brown surface that otherwise develops over time in the environment. The thickness of the oxide layer determines the color of the surface finish. Both Nordic Brown surface finishes are useful to minimize hand and other construction marks which can occur for a short time after the installation of bright Nordic Standard copper. Nordic Brown can also be combined with other surface finishes such as Nordic Standard or Nordic Royal to create various visual effects. Ongoing changes will continue over time with all Nordic Products depending on the local environment. Nordic Brown Light gradually changes over time to a stable dark chocolate brown.

    Nordic Royal is an alloy of copper, aluminum and zinc, with a rich golden through-color. Nordic Royal can also be combined with other surface finishes, e.g. Nordic Bronze or Nordic Brown to create various visual effects. Ongoing changes will continue over time with all Nordic Products depending on the local environment. The material has a thin protective oxide layer which thickens when exposed to the atmosphere. As a result, the surface retains its golden color and simply loses some of its sheen as the oxide layer thickens with exposure to the atmosphere to give a matte finish.
    Nordic Green material is pre-patinated at the Aurubis mill to immediately provide the same green patina that naturally develops over time in the environment. Different intensities of patina flecks can be created revealing some of the dark-oxidized background material, enabling architects to determine the type and intensity of the green patina with choices of Nordic Green Living surface finishes. Aurubis also develops special individual levels of patination to meet the design requirements or to match historically patinated copper on existing buildings. Nordic Green surface finishes can be combined with each other or with other surface finishes to create various visual impacts, e.g. with Nordic Royal. Ongoing changes will continue over time with all Nordic Products depending on the local environment. As a result, just like natural patina, Nordic Green and Nordic Blue undergo continuous changes through environmental exposure dependent upon local atmospheric and rainfall conditions.
